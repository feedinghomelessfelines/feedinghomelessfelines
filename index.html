About Us

We feed hundreds of cats each week in South Florida

  • Adoption – we search for and find suitable families in Florida and throughout the U.S. who are willing to make a homeless feline an integral part of their family.
  • Medical Services – we care for and provide veterinary services for injured or sick cats. Whether they’ve been slashed or bitten, broken bones or are suffering from afflictions, if we can trap them we’ll get them needed medical attention.
  • Spay and Neuter – we continue to search out colonies of homeless felines , trap them and provide spay and neuter services, shots and necessary medical care. If they are adoptable, we’ll work to find them homes.
